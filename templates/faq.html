Frequently Asked Questions
What is ColdMail?

ColdMail is an AI prompt optimizer. We help you write better prompts for AI image/video generators (Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Stable Diffusion, Veo, Runway, Sora) and AI app builders (Cursor, v0, Bolt, Lovable, Replit Agent). Better prompts = better results.

What tools are available?

ColdMail offers two tools: 1) Image & Video Prompt Optimizer — improve prompts for any AI image or video generation model, with a dropdown to select your specific tool. 2) App Builder Prompt Optimizer — improve prompts for AI coding tools like Cursor, v0, and Bolt.

Which AI models are supported?

For image & video: Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Stable Diffusion XL, Google Veo, Runway Gen-3, OpenAI Sora, Flux, Leonardo AI, and Ideogram. For app builders: Cursor, v0, Bolt, Lovable, and Replit Agent.

How does the optimizer work?

Paste your prompt, select your AI model, and hit optimize. Our AI analyzes your prompt for key elements (style, lighting, composition for images; tech stack, features, UI for apps) and produces an enhanced version tuned for your specific model.

What are UGC templates?

UGC (User Generated Content) templates are proven prompt structures for creating marketing content — product shots, testimonials, lifestyle imagery, and before/after comparisons. They're designed for marketers and creators who want to use AI for product promotion and brand awareness.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Is my data private?

Absolutely. We do not store your prompts after analysis. Your content is processed in real-time and immediately discarded. We never share your data.
