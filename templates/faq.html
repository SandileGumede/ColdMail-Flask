Frequently Asked Questions
How does the tool analyze my emails?

We use rule-based checks (spam triggers, personalization markers) + lightweight AI. No data is stored after analysis.

Why is my improved email blank/weird?

This happens with extremely short emails. Try adding context (e.g., 'Hi [Name], I saw your work at [Company]...').

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Analyze 3 emails/day for free. Upgrade to unlock unlimited use and PDF reports.

Can I get a refund if I don't get more replies?

We offer 7-day refunds if the tool malfunctions. Results depend on your email list quality - we can't guarantee replies.

Does this work for cold DMs as well as emails?

Yes! The tool analyzes both cold DMs and cold emails for best practices and spam triggers.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. We do not store your cold DMs or emails after analysis. Your content is never shared.

What counts as an analysis for the free tier?

Each time you submit a cold DM or cold email for analysis, it counts toward your daily free limit.

How do I unlock unlimited analyses?

Click the "Unlock Unlimited Analyses" button on the homepage and follow the instructions to upgrade.

Can I export my results?

Paid users can export their analysis as a PDF report from the results page.

Who can I contact for support?

Use the Contact page to reach out. We're happy to help!
