Frequently Asked Questions
How does the tool analyze my content?

We use rule-based checks (spam triggers, personalization markers, prompt structure) + lightweight AI. No data is stored after analysis.

What tools are available?

ColdMail offers two powerful tools: 1) Cold DM/Email Analyzer for optimizing your outreach messages, and 2) Prompt Improver for developers and vibe coders to get better results from AI app builders like Cursor, v0, Bolt, and Lovable.

Why is my improved content blank/weird?

This happens with extremely short content. Try adding more context for better results.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! You get 6 free analyses total (shared between both tools). Upgrade to unlock up to 200 analyses per month and PDF reports.

Can I get a refund if I don't get more replies?

We offer 7-day refunds if the tool malfunctions. Results depend on your content quality - we can't guarantee specific outcomes.

Does this work for cold DMs as well as emails?

Yes! The email analyzer works for both cold DMs and cold emails for best practices and spam triggers.

How does the Prompt Improver work?

The Prompt Improver is designed for developers and vibe coders using AI app builders. It analyzes your prompts for tech stack specifications, feature details, UI/UX requirements, functionality descriptions, and styling preferences. It then generates an optimized prompt that will get you better code and features from tools like Cursor, v0, Bolt, Lovable, and Replit Agent.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. We do not store your content after analysis. Your data is never shared.

What counts as an analysis for the free tier?

Each time you submit content for analysis (whether it's an email or a prompt), it counts toward your 6 free analyses total.

Click the "Upgrade" button on the homepage and follow the instructions. Paid users get up to 200 analyses per month.

Paid users can export their analysis as a PDF report from the results page.
