üîß Flask Integration

To integrate this dark mode into your Flask app:

  1. Copy dark-mode.css and dark-mode.js to your static/ folder
  2. Include both files in your base template:
<link rel="stylesheet" href="/static/dark-mode.css">
<script src="/static/dark-mode.js"></script>

The theme toggle will be automatically inserted as a menu item in your hamburger navigation. On mobile, it appears as a prominent button in the menu. The system respects user preferences and provides a seamless experience.
